Patient: I haven't slept through the night in maybe six months. He gets up wandering. Last week he made it out the front door before I caught him.

Patient: I came to this appointment because my neighbor said she'd sit with him for an hour. Otherwise I don't go anywhere.

Patient: Some days I just sit in the car in the driveway and cry before I go inside. I don't know how much longer I can do this alone.
