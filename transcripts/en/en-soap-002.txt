Subjective: 35-year-old female presents with sore throat and low-grade fever for 3 days. She reports difficulty swallowing and mild headache. No cough or nasal congestion. She took ibuprofen with some relief. No sick contacts. Objective: Temperature 100.8, heart rate 88, blood pressure 118/74. Oropharynx shows tonsillar exudates bilaterally, tender anterior cervical lymphadenopathy. Rapid strep test positive. Assessment: Streptococcal pharyngitis. Plan: Amoxicillin 500 mg three times daily for 10 days, supportive care, may return to work after 24 hours on antibiotics.
